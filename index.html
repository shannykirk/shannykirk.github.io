About

Shannon Kirkpatrick is a designer, illustrator, and visual journalist. This year, she was chosen as the 2022 SND Student Designer of the Year.

She draws her inspiration from her interests in art, architecture, film, and an endless love of travel.

Currently, she is a senior graphic design student at the S.I. Newhouse School of Public Communications at Syracuse University, with minors in Architecture and Film Studies. Since 2019, she has worked at her schoolâ€™s independent and award-winning newspaper, The Daily Orange.
