ABOUT ME

My story

Hello, I’m Shannon—a multi-disciplinary designer focusing on editorial, illustrative, and immersive design. Above all, I tell stories through design.

I draw my inspiration from architecture, fine arts, and nature.

I honed my editorial design skills at the Star Tribune, The Daily Orange, and the Syracuse Stand. Whenever I get the chance, I love designing for good causes, such as the various non-profit organizations I worked with through the annual Pixels & Print workshop.

Last year, I was chosen as the 2022 SND Student Designer of the Year.

Currently, I am in Cape Town, South Africa working as a graphic design intern at the Kolisi Foundation. Recently, I graduated with a Bachelor of Science degree in Graphic Design from Syracuse University.
